Names of inverse properties

Inverses are not named for all SSN object properties. Inverse properties are provided primarily where they are outbound from classes whose individual members are expected to commonly be the focus of a dataset.

However, the absence of defined inverses can lead to a risk to interoperability. Modelers may be motivated to assert the inverse of any SSN property using inconsistent terms. To offset this, here we recommend terms for inverse properties that are not currently included in the SSN Ontology.
